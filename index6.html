Nervous System

Control • Communication • Direction (Brain • Spinal cord • Nerves)

Obi overlay
Aláfíà
○○○○ “mind is clear / proceed”
Nervous system overview (diagram).

Practice (3 steps)

  • SGI Pillar

    Infinite Wisdom (Tehuti) — perception, interpretation, decision

  • Healing Plant Type

    Restorative (Nervine/Tonic) — rebuild the signal network. Examples: oatstraw, gotu kola, ashwagandha.

  • Obi (4-cowrie) layer

    Aláfíà (○○○○) — clarity/peace confirmation → “proceed with clean mind.”

  • Ifá concept

    Orí (inner guidance & destiny navigation)

  • Key Teaching

    The nervous system is the body’s oracle network: signal → meaning → direction.

Reflection: When you seek guidance, are your signals calm (Aláfíà) or noisy?

Circulatory (Cardiovascular) System

Transport • Life-flow (Heart • Blood • Vessels)

Obi overlay
Ejìfé
○○●● “balance / reciprocity”
Circulatory system (arteries & veins).

Practice (3 steps)

  • SGI Pillar

    Radical Devotion (Auset) — devotion as consistent life-flow

  • Healing Plant Type

    Nutritive — enrich the blood + strengthen life-flow. Examples: nettle, moringa, alfalfa.

  • Obi (4-cowrie) layer

    Ejìfé (○○●●) — balance/reciprocity → “keep the flow even; give & receive.”

  • Ifá concept

    Ẹjẹ (blood as covenant of life)

  • Key Teaching

    What circulates within you becomes your atmosphere—devotion strengthens; bitterness thickens.

Reflection: Where are you out of balance—overgiving, underreceiving, or blocked flow?

Respiratory System

Breath • Oxygen exchange (Airways • Lungs • Diaphragm)

Obi overlay
Ètàwà
○○○● “yes, but adjust one thing”
Respiratory system (airways & lungs).

Practice (3 steps)

  • SGI Pillar

    Words of Power (Sekert) — speech as structured breath

  • Healing Plant Type

    Mucilaginous — soothe airways + coat irritation. Examples: marshmallow root, slippery elm, mullein, aloe.

  • Obi (4-cowrie) layer

    Ètàwà (○○○●) — “yes, but” → one restriction changes the whole breath; adjust the condition.

  • Ifá concept

    Ẹmi (breath/spirit) • vibration, prayer, incantation

  • Key Teaching

    Breath is the bridge: body ↔ mind ↔ spirit. Change the breath, change the state.

Reflection: What is your “one thing” today—pace, posture, or the words you speak?

4

Digestive System

Breakdown • Absorption • Elimination (Mouth • Stomach • Intestines • Liver)

Obi overlay
Òkànràn
○●●● “caution / clean the input”
Digestive tract (overview).

Practice (3 steps)

  • SGI Anchor

    Devotion as discipline — what you ingest becomes your instrument

  • Healing Plant Type

    Bitter (Alterative) — cleanse input, move stagnation, strengthen assimilation. Examples: dandelion, yellow dock, bitter melon, artichoke leaf.

  • Obi (4-cowrie) layer

    Òkànràn (○●●●) — caution/obstacle → “clean the input, simplify, release what irritates.”

  • Ifá concept

    Aṣẹ (transformative power) • input → transformation → assimilation

  • Key Teaching

    Digestion is alchemy: input → transformation → assimilation → release.

Reflection: What are your hidden “irritants”—foods, media, people, or habits?

Musculoskeletal System

Obi overlay
Òyèkú
●●●● “stop / stabilize / ancestral foundation”
Skeletal framework (front view).
Muscular system (overview).

Practice (3 steps)

  • SGI Principle

    Standing on the shoulders of enlightened ancestors — bones as ancestral architecture

  • Healing Plant Type

    Aromatic (Volatile) — warm the structure, move stiffness, improve circulation to tissue. Examples: ginger, rosemary, cinnamon, peppermint.
